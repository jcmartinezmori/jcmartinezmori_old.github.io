About

I am a first year PhD student in Systems at Cornell University. My advisor is Samitha Samaranayake. My research interests lie in the intersection of transportation systems engineering and theoretical computer science. I am interested in developing (or simply using) state-of-the-art algorithms for transportation-related applications. On the theory side, I am interested in online algorithms, approximation algorithms, and parametrized algorithms. On the applications side, I am interested in incorporating a demand-responsive component in public transportation. In particular, I am interested in i) the integration of public transportation and mobility on-demand, and ii) the operations of a bus line with pop-up stops.
